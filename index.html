Welcome to Brakit!


What started as a quarantine conversation evolved into this collection of bracket-based games.


Play alone. Play with friends. Have Fun!

How to Play

A little bit about the layout

Each Brakit is a knockout tournament with sixty-four competitors. The tournaments are divided into four regions, which each region usually having a theme. The top two picks in each region will meet in final region, taking part in the three final rounds (Elite 8, Final 4, Championship).

Making Picks

For each fixture, click on your preferred option, and it will advance to the next round. All the picks are subjective. Use your own judgment, preferences, and life experiences to choose. Some Brakits have hover photos to help remind you of what the option is; the musical ones have a Spotify widget with a playlist of all of the songs featured on the Brakit, just in case you need a refresher.

Final Region

Once you made your picks for all four regions, scroll down to the championship region and pick your winning option. When you pick an overall winner, click on it, and an alert box will appear confirming your winning pick.

Downloading Results

Once you have clicked on your winner, wait about three seconds after this. Then you can download screenshots of each region by clicking the region names at the bottom of the page.

Ready to get Started?

Go to the menu at the top of the page, and pick a Brakit from the categories given. Want to do our latest Brakit? That's right here for you to select!
