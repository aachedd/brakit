Welcome to Brakit!


What started as a quarantine conversation evolved into this collection of bracket-based games.


Play alone. Play with friends. Proceed with caution.

How to Play

A little bit about the layout

Each braket is a decontructed version of a March Madness 64 team bracket. There are 4 regions in each bracket, and a championship region. Each of the 4 regions consist of 16 unique options. These options will faceoff head-to-head, where you will need to pick which option you prefer.

Making Picks

Click on the option you prefer, and it will advance to the next round. All the picks are sujective. Use your own judgement in deterimining which you prefer. Some brackets have hover photos to help remind you of what the option is, and the musical ones have a spotify widget that has a the playlist of all of the songs on the bracket, in case you need a refresher.

Final Region

Once you made picks for all 4 regions, scroll down to the championship region, and ultimately pick your winning option. When you pick an overall winner, click on that, and an alert box will appear confirming your winning pick

Downloading Results

Once you have clicked on your winner, wait about 3 seconds after this, and then you can download screenshots of each region by clicking the region names at the bottom of the page.

Ready to get Started?

Go to the menu at the top of the page, and pick a bracket from the categories given. Want to do our latest bracket? That's right here for you to select!
